Melbourne's public transport is an essential way for people to travel and live in the city. Trams, trains and buses make up an excellent public transport network.

City Circle tram service

city circle stop city circle route
Click to see more info

The City Circle tram is a free and convenient way to see the major attractions of Melbourne's city centre during your journey. Major attractions include the Melbourne Museum, Parliament House, State Library Victoria, Docklands, Federation Square, SEA Life Melbourne Aquarium and the Princess Theatre.

It includes 28 stops, covering most of the travel needs of people living and working in Melbourne's CBD.

City Circle tram service

The City Circle tram service operates within Melbourne's central business district. The service operates in a circular route passing major tourist attractions, as well as linking with other tram, train and bus routes in and around Melbourne.
